About

Namadhu Karangal

Namadhu Karangal is a capital trust which started at 2018. And later, was registered at 27th of May,2022. The trust was a initiative of Subramaniyam Mahalingam with his own income. Namadhu Karangal helped 25-30 students financially for their education. These students were from Kindergarden to Degree Graduates. Namadhu karangal succesfully helped two students to become a graduate.

Namadhu Karangal needed financial backup, since there was curfew because of Covid-19. So, Subramaniyam .M started a convenience shop named "Idhu Namma Kada" in 2020.

Till now Namadhu Karangal was functional with the funds raised by Mr.Subramaniyam and the income of the convenience shop.

About

Idhu Namma Kada

Idhu Namma Kada(INK) was started at 2020, for the sole motive to raise funds for the serivces done by Namadhu karangal. After being succesful in getting funds for Namadhu Karangal, many social activities were provided by Idhu Namma Kada during the lockdown.

To those people who were suffering due to the curfew, INK provided them with free foods during the hard times of Covid-19. Even after covid, INK was continuing to provide food for many Old-age Homes during many special events.
