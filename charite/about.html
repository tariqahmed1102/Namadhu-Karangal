About Founder

Subramaniam M., BCom, MBA

Subramaniam Mahalingam is the founder of Namadhu karangal and Idhu Namma Kada. MR. Subramaniyam started both of these with his own savings to help and support the children of the society who were struggling financially for education. The donation was started with a single child. Later was built as a trust which helps many children, elders, people with medical conditions and old-age homes as well.
How it started ?
Mr. Subramaniam was born into a middle-class family in India. He always believed that education was the key to a better life. After graduating from college, he decided to start a trust to help children in need get an education. He named the trust Namadhu Karangal, which means "Our Strength" in Tamil.
Namadhu Karangal provides educational opportunities to children from low-income families and also supports the ill people fighting deadly diseases. The trust offers scholarships, and help with school supplies. It also offers elders the support they need. And Idhu Namma Kada offers food for the needed with low cost affordable by them. Namadhu Karangal has helped thousands of children get an education and improve their lives.
Mr. Subramaniam is a man who believes in the power of education to change lives. Namadhu Karangal is a testament to his dedication and commitment to helping others.

About

Idhu Namma Kada

Idhu Namma Kada(INK) was started at 2020, for the sole motive to raise funds for the serivces done by Namadhu karangal. After being succesful in getting funds for Namadhu Karangal, many social activities were provided by Idhu Namma Kada during the lockdown.
To those people who were suffering due to the curfew, INK provided them with free foods during the hard times of Covid-19. Even after covid, INK was continuing to provide food for many Old-age Homes during many special events.
